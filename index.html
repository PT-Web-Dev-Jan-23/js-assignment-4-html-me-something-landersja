Understanding My Journey Up to This Point

You know the type of person who is into any and everything? They usually know one fact or another about the most random subjects. However when it comes to mastering in a field they never dip more than a two below the water's surface. Then 27 years later they're having a quater-life crisis about what is the next step in their life? You can't see me raising my hand but I am one of them. Hello, my name is Jamyria and I don't know how I got here. Yet, as confusing as it is it all makes so much sense.

Author: Jamyria Landers
